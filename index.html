City Breaks

(ˈsɪtɪ breɪk ) noun. tourism. a short holiday spent in a city. a city break in Paris or Rome.


My favourite hobby is taking a backpack & exploring a new city. If an additional city is within a short commute by train or car, I like to add another adventure into the mix before flying home.

In recent years I have visted Bratislava, Slovakia. Vienna, Austria. Budapest, Hungary. Sofia, Bulgaria. Skopje, North Macedonia & Boston, USA.

My next trip is a return to Reykjavik, Iceland.
